About Touzenken

Touzenken brings the authentic taste of Shanghai street food to Osaka's famous Juso food alley. Our signature pan-fried soup dumplings are handmade daily with fresh ingredients and cooked on a hot iron pan to achieve a crispy bottom and juicy filling.
